- Can cover distances > 2,000 km
- Long-distance migrating species:
    - *Nyctalus noctula*
    - *Nyctalus leisleri*
    - *Pipistrellus nathusii*
    - *Vespertilio murinus*
    - *Nyctalus lasiopterus* ?
- Particularly cover:
    - Coastlines
    - Large rivers
    - Mountain passes
- But are poorly documented (and studies mostly concern *P. nathusii* and *N. noctula*)
